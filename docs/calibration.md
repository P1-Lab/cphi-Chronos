<div class="calibration-protocol">
  <h2>Calibration Protocol</h2>

  <section class="moon-phase-calibration">
    <h3>1. Moon Phase Calibration</h3>
    <ol>
      <li>Identify last known new moon date.</li>
      <li>Pull crown to position 2.</li>
      <li>Rotate until moon disc is fully dark.</li>
      <li>Advance by number of days since new moon.</li>
    </ol>
  </section>

  <section class="tide-calibration">
    <h3>2. Tide Calibration</h3>
    <ol>
      <li>Connect via internal USB-C.</li>
      <li>Edit <code>config.h</code> (Amplitude, Phase, MSL).</li>
      <li>Flash firmware.</li>
    </ol>
  </section>
</div>
